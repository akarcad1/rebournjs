Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Have questions? We've got answers! Here are some frequently asked questions about RebornXP:

  1. How can I change the wallpaper in RebornXP?

    2. Open Start Menu, navigate to personalization, click on change wallpaper. Choose from default stock wallpapers or add your own from a direct URL

  2. Can I install applications from sources other than the App Store?

    3. You can develop your own programs for RebornXP using this template, and also upload from your Computer - but we do not recommend executing unsigned JS which may harm your OS

  3. How do I create a new user account in RebornXP?

    4. Open Start Menu, navigate to User Accounts & Control - Choose options from changing your name, profile picture or add a new account

  4. Is there a way to backup and restore my RebornXP settings?

    5. Currently the data on RebornXP is stored in your Web Browser as persistent cache storage, and all your data will be erased if you clear the site data. Unfortunately there are no options to backup your data, but however, you can always download your files to your local OS
